About Block

Block is an easy-to-use community and trading token on the Solana blockchain. It is based on the idea that operations with cryptocurrencies should be accessible to everyone without exception and be truly convenient and efficient.

How to buy

Becoming a Block token holder is very simple:

  1. make sure that there is a sufficient amount of USDC in your wallet on the Solana blockchain;
  2. make a transaction to the address 6WbkC6Dtz5iSCzR5AbJ4QxntfUGrHMLAJiJRUNgFCEaH  of the corresponding amount in USDC to purchase the desired amount of Block tokens:
    • 100 USDC to get 2000 BLOCK;
    • 200 USDC to get 4000 BLOCK;
    • 400 USDC to get 8000 BLOCK;
  3. after successful completion of the transaction and verification by our moderators, you will receive the desired number of tokens.
